# Lessons Learned from CPS and Cable Analysis

## Cable mechanical properties are…

…not well understood.

## For installation, storm and fatigue analysis, bending stiffness is the key input - It defines the structure’s resistance to bending loads
## In practice bend stiffness is dependent on: - curvature and rate-of-change of curvature - tension - temperature

Most cable suppliers define bend stiffness like this:

–

1.5kNm2
